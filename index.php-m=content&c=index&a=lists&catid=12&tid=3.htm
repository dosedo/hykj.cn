  • 资深产品经理
    Location: Xiamen

     

    Company Description:

    eHealth China (Xiamen) Technology Co., Ltd. (eHealth China) was established in Xiamen, China in 2003, as a wholly owned foreign enterprise operating in China. eHealth China is a wholly owned subsidiary of eHealth Inc.

     

    eHealth, Inc. headquarters in Silicon Valley in the United States. As the country’s largest online health insurance platform, eHealth, Inc. is an internet and technology company that makes it easier for everyone to find affordable, quality health insurance, every day.

     

    Through eHealthInsurance.com, eHealth has helped over 5 million Americans find the quality health insurance products that best meet their personal needs and budget. eHealth gives customers all the tools they need to make shopping for plans simpler, more pleasant, and more productive.

     

    The Team

    You won’t be alone on the Product team. We have decades of experience on the team in both Xiamen and US offices. You have lots of opportunities to work with world-class product managers in Silicon Valley, who joined us from a range of leading companies such as Amazon, Apple, Disney, IAC, Hallmark, Macy’s, Walgreens, Jawbone, and more.

     

    We provide a highly customer-centric, team-oriented environment in our offices with a culture that:

    · demands authentic empathy for our customers

    · is excited by the business mission of the company

    · runs on collaboration with other disciplines

    · requires you to come up with ideas on your own

    · has strong and opinionated leadership pushing the department to evolve

    · supports PMs in having long and passionate careers

    · recognizes the need to have a balanced life that includes family, friends, health and wellness

    · demands authenticity

    · seeks to humanize technology in a most challenging product category

     

    Position Summary

    We are looking for an experienced Product Manager. The successful candidate will be responsible for building platform products that work for multiple business units’ customer facing products and external partners.

     

    What you’ll be doing

    · Work collaboratively with other product managers, architect designers and developers to make scalable platform service that work for multiple business units’ consumer facing products and even for external partners

    · Develop, prioritize, and manage product roadmap/backlogs and stakeholder requests

    · Pitch product ideas to cross-functional groups

    · Write product specs and user stories

    · “Own” your work and be a leader at the company

    · Track and measure the success of your products
     

    Basic Qualifications:

    · 8+ years product management experience

    · Ability to lead projects and work in a cross functional team environment

    · Proven track record of taking ownership and driving results on projects

    · Strong strategic thinking and prioritization skills

    · Details oriented, willing to get down to every level of details

    · Excellent oral and written communication skills in English

    · Customer centric thinking

     

    Preferred Qualifications

    · Has engineering background

    · Has project management skills

    · Experience delivering complex products via Agile / Scrum development processes
